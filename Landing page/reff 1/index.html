Ora

Ora Beach is a beach located on Seram Island, North Seram sub-district, Central Maluku, Maluku, Indonesia. The beach is located at the western end of Sawai Bay, next to Saleman Village and Sawai Village, on the edge of the Manusela National Park forest.

Slili

Slili Beach has another name, Watu Lawang Beach and also Cinta Slili. Watu Lawang Beach is named because there is a small island commonly referred to as a coral hill at the bottom of a basin, about 500 meters from Sundak Beach.
